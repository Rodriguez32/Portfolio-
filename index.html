About Me

My name is Ilda Rodriguez, a growing software developer. I began my tech journey in 2018, doing a Full Stack Coding Bootcamp from Northwestern University in Evanston. I am passionate about front-end development and continue to learn UX/UI and testing. Currently I am one of the facilitators at Kidz Kosmos a virtual place for kids to learn about personal development, coding and leadership. I also volunteer with ChickTech and an after school STEM Class at my kids elementary School. As a Latina in tech I want children to learn about tech and help close the gender gap.
