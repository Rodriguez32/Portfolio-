About Me

My name is Ilda Rodriguez, a growing software developer. I began my tech journey in 2018, doing a Full Stack Coding Bootcamp from Northwestern University in Evanston. I am passionate about front-end development and continue to study UX/UI and software testing.
Over the summer I was as a facilitator for the summer coding camp at Kidz Kosmos - a virtual place for kids to learn about personal development, coding and leadership. I have also volunteered with ChickTech and Boy Scouts of America - STEAM Camp. As a mom and Latina in tech I want children to learn about the different career paths in tech and help close the gender gap.
